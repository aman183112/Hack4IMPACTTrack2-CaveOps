# 🏥 NirogPath

> AI-powered multilingual health triage assistant for rural India

**Team:** CaveOps  
**Member:** Aman Polai  
**Hackathon:** Hack4IMPACT — Track 2  
**Theme:** Smart Technology for a Sustainable World  
**Domain:** AI & Automation in Smart Healthcare

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

India has over 600 million rural citizens with little to no access to a
doctor's first opinion. Patients travel 20–40 km to overcrowded hospitals
for conditions a local PHC could handle — simply because they can't assess
symptom severity. Existing tools like WebMD are English-only, appointment-
based, and built around Western diseases — completely missing India-specific
conditions like dengue, typhoid, and kala-azar.

---

## 💡 Solution

NirogPath is a conversational AI triage web app that acts as an intelligent
first point of contact between a rural patient and the healthcare system.

- 🗣️ User describes symptoms in **English or Hindi**
- 🤖 AI conducts a **dynamic follow-up interview** based on responses
- 🟢🟡🔴 Outputs a **severity flag** with plain-language explanation
- 🏥 Recommends the **right level of care** (self-care → PHC → Hospital)
- 📍 Shows the **nearest facility** based on pincode
